Overview

YU Zmanim is committed to protecting your privacy. The app is designed to operate without collecting personal information.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names or contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Analytics data
  • Usage tracking information

No accounts are required to use the app.

Minyan Participation Feature

The app includes an optional feature allowing users to indicate participation in a minyan. This feature uses Apple CloudKit to update a shared numerical counter within the app.

  • Participation is anonymous.
  • No identifying information is displayed.
  • The developer does not maintain independent participation records.
  • All related infrastructure is managed by Apple.

Notifications

If enabled, notifications are delivered using Apple Push Notification Service. Users may disable notifications at any time in device settings.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking technologies.

Children's Privacy

YU Zmanim does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Policy Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by the Effective Date above.
