1. Information We Collect

YU Zmanim may collect limited technical information needed to operate core features, including notification preferences, app installation identifiers, and minyan-help request interactions.

We use this information to:

  • Deliver app notifications you opt into.
  • Sync minyan-help requests and responses.
  • Improve reliability and troubleshoot service issues.

3. Push Notifications

If enabled, notifications may include schedule updates and minyan-help alerts. You can disable notifications anytime in iOS Settings.

4. Cloud Services

Some app features use Apple CloudKit to sync records across devices. Data handling for iCloud services is subject to Apple terms and privacy policies.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We only share data with service providers required to operate the app (for example, Apple services used for iOS functionality).

6. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as reasonably necessary to provide app functionality, maintain security, and comply with legal obligations.

7. Your Choices

  • Disable notifications in iOS Settings.
  • Uninstall the app to stop further collection through the app.

8. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13.
